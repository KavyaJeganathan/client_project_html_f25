Welcome to the UMSI Career Development Resource Hub

Your Central Hub for Career Success at UMSI

The UMSI Career Development Office (CDO) supports MSI students through every stage of their career journey, from early exploration to full-time job offers. Our team offers strategic, personalized guidance to help you build the tools, confidence, and connections needed for long-term success. We don't just help you "find a job." We partner with you to develop career clarity, build real-world experience, and navigate the job search with intention. Whether you're applying to your first internship or preparing for a second career, we're here for your support.

Why Use This Resource?

  • Everything in One Place: Stop searching endlessly—quickly access the resources you need for resumes, interviews, networking, mentorship, and more.
  • Student-Focused Navigation: Browse by topic or question. Key areas are highlighted so you can get moving right away.
  • Guidance Backed by Experts: All resources are recommended and reviewed by the UMSI Career Development Office, so you can trust the advice and materials you find here.
  • Always Accessible: Visit the site anytime, from any device. We’re committed to meeting the accessibility needs of all students.
  • Modern Support, UMSI Excellence: We benchmark against top peer institutions and integrate UMSI’s unique support, showcasing our commitment to your success.

What We Offer

We offer a wide range of services and resources that are practical, student-focused, and designed to help you build momentum in your career journey:

  • Career Coaching: Meet 1:1 with a professional or peer coach to explore your goals, strengthen your materials, practice interviews, or talk through your questions.
  • Career Courses & Workshops: Build core career skills — from resume writing to salary negotiation — through hands-on sessions and career-related courses.
  • Internship & Job Search Support: Learn strategies for finding and securing meaningful opportunities that align with your interests.
  • Employer Access: Explore job postings, attend events, and meet recruiters through career fairs and networking events.
  • Exclusive Student Tools: Use platforms like CareerLink to book coaching, find opportunities, and track events all in one place.

Getting Started

If you're not sure where to begin, these are great first steps:

  • Visit our guides on resumes, networking, and portfolios to update your materials.
  • Log in to CareerLink to search for jobs, schedule appointments, and register for upcoming events.
  • Stop by our drop-in coaching hours or email us directly at umsi.careers@umich.edu if you need a quick question answered.

FAQs

  • Q: How do I meet with a Career Coach?
    A: You can schedule 1:1 coaching appointments through CareerLink. Drop-in hours are also available for brief questions with no appointment needed. We also have trained peer career coaches ready to support you.
  • Q: Where do I search for internships or jobs?
    A: CareerLink is the first place to check while using filters like "Job Function" to refine your search. We also maintain lists of external job boards by industry, interest area, and format (e.g. remote, part-time, international).
  • Q: How do I prepare for a career fair or recruiting event?
    A: Use our Elevator Pitch Rubric and Career Fair Prep Slides. Attend a prep session beforehand, and don't hesitate to ask a coach for help practicing or refining your materials.
